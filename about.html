About SBM Apparels Pvt Limited

Pioneering sustainable textile manufacturing with a commitment to quality, ethics, and environmental responsibility

Our Background

SBM Apparels Pvt Limited has established itself as a trusted name in the textile manufacturing industry, based in the textile hub of Tirupur, Tamil Nadu, India. With over 15 years of dedicated experience, we have consistently delivered excellence in custom clothing solutions for B2B buyers, exporters, fashion brands, and corporate clients worldwide.

Our journey has been driven by a passion for quality, innovation, and sustainable practices. We have invested in state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and skilled workforce to ensure that every product meets international standards while maintaining environmental consciousness.

Today, we serve clients across 50+ countries, with a portfolio that includes fashion brands, corporate giants, and export-focused businesses who trust us for their textile requirements.

Sustainable Manufacturing Practices

Our commitment to responsible textile production

Eco-Friendly Operations

At SBM Apparels, sustainability is not a marketing initiative—it's the foundation of our business. We have implemented comprehensive programs that reduce environmental impact across every stage of production.

  • Recycled Material Sourcing: We prioritize sourcing recycled textiles and sustainable fibers, reducing reliance on virgin materials.
  • Water Conservation: Our manufacturing facilities employ advanced water-efficient technologies to minimize consumption.
  • Waste Reduction: We have implemented systematic waste management and recycling programs throughout our operations.
  • Energy Efficiency: We invest in energy-efficient equipment and renewable energy sources to power our facilities.
  • Chemical Management: We use eco-friendly dyes and chemicals that meet international environmental standards.
  • Carbon Footprint Reduction: We continuously track and reduce our carbon emissions through process optimization.
